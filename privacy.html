Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication and Empowerment Foundation (“EJPEEF”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our donors, volunteers, partners, and visitors.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and donation details when you interact with our website or participate in our programs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate with you about our projects and activities.
  • To process donations and send receipts.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Protection

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers or legal authorities when required by law.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and store preferences. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us via email at info@ejpeef.org.
