Our Story

Founded in 2018, Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication and Empowerment Foundation (EJPEEF) was established with a heartfelt mission — to break the cycle of poverty by empowering the most vulnerable groups in society. Through education, vocational training, and sustainable livelihood programs, EJPEEF has impacted over 2,500 lives across more than 150 communities in Nigeria.

From supporting widows and orphans to mentoring young entrepreneurs, every initiative we lead is driven by compassion and the belief that no one should be left behind. Together, we are building a nation where every person has a chance to thrive with dignity and hope.

Why Donate?

Investing in the skills and education of rural women, young adults, elderly, disadvantaged people, and persons with disabilities is crucial for sustainable development. Support our initiatives to empower communities, ensuring everyone has the tools and confidence to thrive.
