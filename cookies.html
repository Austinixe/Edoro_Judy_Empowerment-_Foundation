Cookies Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication and Empowerment Foundation (“EJPEEF”) uses cookies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help improve your browsing experience and website functionality.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as navigation and donation processing.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors use our website to improve user experience.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings such as language and display preferences.

3. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

4. Updates to This Policy

EJPEEF may update this Cookies Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last Updated” date.

For more information, contact us at info@ejpeef.org.
