About Edoror Judy Foundation

Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives — Our journey toward building a Nigeria where every individual has access to education, opportunity, and hope.

About the Founder

My name is Mr. Edoror Osemudiamen, the CEO of Robleodom Investment Ltd and Founder of the Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication & Empowerment Foundation (EJPEEF). I am an empowerment visionist — and because of my passion for community growth, I was appointed as the Anti-Poverty and Empowerment Program Director in Esan West Local Government Council of Edo State by Hon. Godsent Agboibo, the Chairman of the Council.

As Director, I successfully promoted initiatives such as the donation of educational materials, waste bins, vocational skills training, and other empowerment classes aimed at community development.

Our foundation’s mission is to break the cycle of poverty — not just by providing immediate relief, but by equipping vulnerable individuals and communities with the tools, skills, and resources needed for long-term independence and sustainable well-being.

Empowerment fosters self-confidence, personal growth, and accountability. Through this process, we create lasting, positive transformations that benefit individuals, organizations, and society as a whole.

We also provide a supportive framework for individuals with a spirit of giving — both within Nigeria and in the diaspora — to directly support their communities. Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication & Empowerment Foundation ensures that your donations or empowerment projects are delivered according to your intentions and directives.

Who We Are

Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication and Empowerment Foundation (EJPEEF) is a Nigerian non-governmental organization dedicated to eradicating poverty and empowering marginalized groups. We focus on education, vocational training, healthcare, and sustainable development projects for women, youth, the elderly, and people with disabilities.

Our Mission

To provide access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities that promote dignity and self-reliance for every Nigerian.

Our Vision

To build a nation free from poverty and inequality — where empowered citizens lead their communities toward lasting progress.

Our Values

Integrity, Compassion, Empowerment, Innovation, and Community Service form the foundation of all our efforts.
