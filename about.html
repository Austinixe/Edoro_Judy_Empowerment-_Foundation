About Edoror Judy Foundation

Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives — Our journey toward building a Nigeria where every individual has access to education, opportunity, and hope.

Who We Are

Edoror Judy Poverty Eradication and Empowerment Foundation (EJPEEF) is a Nigerian non-governmental organization dedicated to eradicating poverty and empowering marginalized groups. We focus on education, vocational training, healthcare, and sustainable development projects for women, youth, the elderly, and people with disabilities.

Our Mission

To provide access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities that promote dignity and self-reliance for every Nigerian.

Our Vision

To build a nation free from poverty and inequality — where empowered citizens lead their communities toward lasting progress.

Our Values

Integrity, Compassion, Empowerment, Innovation, and Community Service form the foundation of all our efforts.
